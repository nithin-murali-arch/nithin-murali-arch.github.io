Breads & Tiffins

Chapati (சப்பாத்தி)

Ingredients:
  • Wheat Flour - 2 cups
  • Warm Water - ~1 cup
  • Oil/Ghee - 2 tsp
  • Salt - 1/2 tsp
Instructions:
  1. Mix flour and salt. Gradually add warm water and knead into a soft dough.
  2. Add oil/ghee and knead for another 5 minutes. Cover and rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Make small balls and roll into thin circles.
  4. Cook on a hot tawa, flipping twice, pressing gently to puff up.

Aloo Paratha (உருளைக்கிழங்கு பராத்தா)

Ingredients:
  • For Dough: Use Chapati dough recipe
  • For Filling: 3 boiled potatoes, 1 onion (chopped), 1 green chilli (chopped), Spices
Instructions:
  1. Mash potatoes and mix with onion, chilli, and spices.
  2. Take a ball of dough, flatten, place filling, and seal.
  3. Roll out gently into a slightly thick circle.
  4. Cook on a tawa with ghee until golden and crisp.

Poori (பூரி)

Ingredients:
  • Wheat Flour - 2 cups
  • Rava/Sooji - 2 tbsp (for crispiness)
  • Salt, Water, Oil for frying
Instructions:
  1. Knead a stiff dough using flour, rava, salt, and water. Rest for 15 minutes.
  2. Roll into small, thick discs.
  3. Deep fry in hot oil, pressing gently to puff up.
  4. Fry until golden and drain. Serve immediately.

Rava Kichadi (ரவா கிச்சடி)

Ingredients:
  • Rava (Semolina) - 1 cup
  • Mixed Vegetables - 1 cup
  • Onion & Tomato - 1 each
  • Ghee, Turmeric, Tempering Spices
Instructions:
  1. Roast rava until aromatic.
  2. In a pan, temper spices in ghee, sauté onions, tomatoes, and vegetables.
  3. Add 3 cups of hot water, salt, and turmeric. Bring to a boil.
  4. Slowly add rava while stirring. Cover and cook on low heat.

Godhuma Rava Upma (கோதுமை ரவா உப்புமா)

Ingredients:
  • Cracked Wheat (Samba Rava) - 1 cup
  • Onion - 1, Green Chillis - 2
  • Tempering Spices, Oil, Salt, Water - 2.5 cups
Instructions:
  1. Temper spices in hot oil, sauté onions and chillis.
  2. Add water and salt, bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat, add cracked wheat slowly, stirring continuously.
  4. Cover and cook on low flame for 10-15 minutes.

Ven Pongal (வெண் பொங்கல்)

Ingredients:
  • Raw Rice - 1 cup
  • Moong Dal - 1/2 cup
  • Ghee - 4 tbsp
  • Ginger, Pepper, Cumin, Cashews
Instructions:
  1. Dry roast moong dal lightly.
  2. Pressure cook rice and dal with 4 cups of water until mushy.
  3. In ghee, temper pepper, cumin, ginger, and cashews.
  4. Pour tempering over the cooked pongal, add salt, and mix well.

Adai (அடை)

Ingredients:
  • Adai Batter (from mix or homemade)
  • Onion, Coriander (optional, chopped)
  • Oil for cooking
Instructions:
  1. If using, mix chopped onion and coriander into the batter.
  2. Heat a tawa, pour a ladle of batter and spread into a thick circle.
  3. Drizzle oil around the edges and in the center.
  4. Cook on both sides until golden brown and crisp.

Semiya Upma (சேமியா உப்புமா)

Ingredients:
  • Vermicelli (Semiya) - 1.5 cups, roasted
  • Mixed Vegetables - 1 cup
  • Onion - 1, Green Chillis - 2
  • Tempering Spices, Oil, Salt
Instructions:
  1. Temper spices, sauté onions, chillis and vegetables.
  2. Add 2.5 cups of water and salt, bring to a boil.
  3. Add roasted vermicelli, stir well, and cook on low heat until water is absorbed.

Arisi Upma (அரிசி உப்புமா)

Ingredients:
  • Raw Rice - 1 cup
  • Toor Dal - 2 tbsp
  • Onion - 1 (optional)
  • Tempering: Mustard, Urad Dal, Red Chilli, Asafoetida
Instructions:
  1. Coarsely grind rice and toor dal.
  2. In a pressure cooker, heat oil, temper spices. Sauté onions if using.
  3. Add 3 cups water and salt. Bring to a boil.
  4. Slowly add the rice mixture, stirring to prevent lumps.
  5. Cook for 2-3 whistles.

Rice Dishes

Vegetable Pulao (வெஜிடபிள் புலாவ்)

Ingredients:
  • Basmati Rice - 1.5 cups, soaked
  • Mixed Vegetables - 2 cups
  • Onion - 1, Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Whole Spices (Cinnamon, Clove, Bay Leaf)
Instructions:
  1. Sauté whole spices in ghee. Add sliced onions and fry until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and vegetables. Sauté for a few minutes.
  3. Add soaked rice, 3 cups of water, and salt.
  4. Cook until rice is fluffy (pressure cooker or pot).

Vegetable Biriyani (வெஜிடபிள் பிரியாணி)

Ingredients:
  • Basmati Rice - 1.5 cups, soaked
  • Mixed Vegetables - 2 cups
  • Onion, Tomato, Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Curd, Mint Leaves, Biryani Masala
Instructions:
  1. Sauté whole spices, then onions. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes, vegetables, and powders.
  2. Stir in curd, mint, and salt. Add soaked rice and water.
  3. Pressure cook for 2 whistles.

Gravies & Curries

Paneer Butter Masala (பனீர் பட்டர் மசாலா)

Ingredients:
  • Paneer - 200g
  • Onion - 2, Tomatoes - 4, Cashews - 15
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste, Spices
  • Kasuri Methi, Butter, Cream
Instructions:
  1. Make a paste of blanched tomatoes and soaked cashews.
  2. Sauté onions and ginger-garlic paste. Add tomato-cashew paste and spices. Cook until oil separates.
  3. Add water, paneer, and kasuri methi. Simmer, finish with cream.

Chana Masala (சன்னா மசாலா)

Ingredients:
  • Chickpeas - 1 cup, boiled
  • Onion - 1, Tomato - 2 (pureed)
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste, Chana Masala Powder
Instructions:
  1. Sauté onions, then ginger-garlic paste. Add tomato puree and spices.
  2. Cook well, then add boiled chickpeas and water.
  3. Simmer for 15 minutes.

Mixed Vegetable Kurma (வெஜிடபிள் குருமா)

Ingredients:
  • Mixed Vegetables - 2 cups
  • Onion & Tomato - 1 each
  • For Paste: Coconut, Cashews, Green Chilli, Spices
Instructions:
  1. Grind the coconut paste.
  2. Sauté whole spices, onions, and tomatoes.
  3. Add vegetables and cook until soft.
  4. Add coconut paste and water. Simmer until thick.

Kadai Paneer (கடாய் பனீர்)

Ingredients:
  • Paneer - 200g, cubed
  • Capsicum, Onion, Tomato - 1 each, cubed
  • Kadai Masala, Ginger-Garlic Paste
Instructions:
  1. Roast and grind whole spices for Kadai Masala.
  2. Sauté cubed onions and capsicum until crisp. Remove.
  3. Make a gravy with onions, tomatoes, ginger-garlic paste, and kadai masala.
  4. Add paneer, sautéed vegetables, and simmer.

Mutter Paneer (மட்டர் பனீர்)

Ingredients:
  • Paneer - 200g
  • Green Peas (Mutter) - 1 cup
  • Onion - 2, Tomato - 3
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste, Spices
Instructions:
  1. Make a puree of onion, ginger, and garlic. Make a separate puree of tomatoes.
  2. Sauté onion puree until golden. Add tomato puree and spices. Cook well.
  3. Add peas and water, cook until soft.
  4. Add paneer cubes and simmer for 5-7 minutes.

Dal Fry (தால் ஃப்ரை)

Ingredients:
  • Toor Dal - 1 cup, pressure cooked
  • Onion & Tomato - 1 each
  • Ginger-Garlic, Spices
  • Tadka: Ghee, Cumin, Mustard, Red Chilli
Instructions:
  1. Sauté onions, ginger-garlic, and tomatoes. Add spices.
  2. Add this masala to the cooked dal and simmer.
  3. Prepare tadka in ghee and pour over the dal before serving.

Kadai Vegetable (கடாய் வெஜிடபிள்)

Ingredients:
  • Mixed Vegetables - 2 cups, cubed
  • Capsicum & Onion - 1 each, cubed
  • Kadai Masala, Tomato Puree
Instructions:
  1. Stir-fry cubed onions and capsicum. Keep aside.
  2. Sauté other vegetables until almost cooked.
  3. Add tomato puree and kadai masala. Cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add the stir-fried vegetables and mix well.

Aloo Masala (பூரி கிழங்கு மசாலா)

Ingredients:
  • Potatoes - 4, boiled & mashed
  • Onion - 2, sliced
  • Green Chilli, Ginger, Turmeric
  • Tempering Spices
Instructions:
  1. Temper spices, sauté onions, green chillis, and ginger.
  2. Add turmeric, mashed potatoes, salt, and 1 cup of water.
  3. Mix well and simmer for 5 minutes until it thickens slightly.

Sambar (சாம்பார்)

Ingredients:
  • Toor Dal - 3/4 cup, cooked
  • Mixed Vegetables (Drumstick, Pumpkin, Brinjal) - 1.5 cups
  • Tamarind extract, Sambar Powder
  • Tempering Spices
Instructions:
  1. Cook vegetables in water with turmeric until tender.
  2. Add tamarind extract, sambar powder, and salt. Boil for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add cooked, mashed dal and bring to a simmer.
  4. Prepare tadka and pour over the sambar.

Dry Vegetables & Sides

Bhindi Masala (வெண்டைக்காய் மசாலா)

Ingredients:
  • Okra (Bhindi) - 250g, chopped
  • Onion - 1, Tomato - 2
  • Spices: Turmeric, Chilli, Coriander powder
Instructions:
  1. Pan-fry bhindi until crisp and keep aside.
  2. Make a masala with onions, tomatoes, and spices.
  3. Add fried bhindi to the masala and mix gently.

Jeera Aloo (சீரக உருளைக்கிழங்கு)

Ingredients:
  • Potatoes - 4, boiled & cubed
  • Cumin Seeds (Jeera) - 1 tbsp
  • Turmeric, Chilli Powder, Asafoetida
Instructions:
  1. Heat oil, add cumin seeds. Once they crackle, add asafoetida.
  2. Add boiled potato cubes, turmeric, chilli powder, and salt.
  3. Toss gently and roast on low heat until potatoes are slightly crisp.

Avial (அவியல்)

Ingredients:
  • Mixed Veggies (Yam, Plantain, Carrot, Beans, Drumstick) - 2 cups
  • Curd - 1/2 cup, sour
  • For Paste: Coconut, Cumin, Green Chilli
  • Coconut Oil
Instructions:
  1. Cook vegetables with turmeric until tender.
  2. Grind coconut paste without water.
  3. Add the paste to cooked vegetables. Mix and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Switch off heat, add beaten curd and a drizzle of coconut oil. Mix well.

Gothsu (கத்திரிக்காய் கொத்சு)

Ingredients:
  • Brinjal - 250g, roasted & mashed
  • Onion - 1, chopped
  • Tamarind extract
  • Tempering Spices
Instructions:
  1. Roast brinjal over a flame, peel skin, and mash the pulp.
  2. Temper spices, sauté onions.
  3. Add tamarind extract and salt. Boil for 5 minutes.
  4. Add mashed brinjal pulp and simmer for another 5 minutes.

Vathakuzhambu (வற்றல் குழம்பு)

Ingredients:
  • Sundakkai Vathal - 2 tbsp
  • Tamarind - lemon sized ball
  • Sambar Powder, Turmeric
  • Tempering Spices, Sesame Oil
Instructions:
  1. Soak tamarind and extract juice.
  2. Fry vathal in a little oil until dark. Keep aside.
  3. Temper spices in sesame oil. Add sambar powder.
  4. Add tamarind extract, salt, and turmeric. Boil until raw smell goes.
  5. Add fried vathal and a piece of jaggery. Simmer until thick.

Potato Roast (உருளைக்கிழங்கு வறுவல்)

Ingredients:
  • Potatoes - 4, boiled & cubed
  • Chilli Powder, Turmeric, Salt
  • Tempering Spices, Oil
Instructions:
  1. Heat oil, temper spices.
  2. Add cubed potatoes, chilli powder, turmeric, and salt.
  3. Roast on medium-low flame, turning occasionally, until potatoes are golden and crisp on all sides.

Cucumber Onion Raita (வெள்ளரி வெங்காய தயிர் பச்சடி)

Ingredients:
  • Thick Curd - 1 cup
  • Cucumber - 1/2, finely chopped
  • Onion - 1 small, finely chopped
  • Coriander leaves, Salt
Instructions:
  1. Whisk the curd until smooth.
  2. Add chopped cucumber, onion, and a pinch of salt.
  3. Mix well and garnish with fresh coriander.
  4. Chill before serving.

Coconut & Tomato Chutney (சட்னி வகைகள்)

Coconut Chutney:
  • Grated Coconut - 1 cup
  • Roasted Chana Dal - 2 tbsp
  • Green Chilli - 2, Ginger - small piece
Tomato Chutney:
  1. Sauté 1 chopped onion, 2 chopped tomatoes, and 2 red chillis in oil until soft.
  2. Cool and grind to a paste with salt.
  3. Temper with mustard seeds and curry leaves.
